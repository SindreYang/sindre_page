小作文

Directions: Write a notice to recruit a student for Prof. Smith’s research project on campus sports activities. Specify the duties and requirements of the job.

说明：写一份通知，为史密斯教授的校园体育活动研究项目招募一名学生。明确工作职责和要求。

Notice:

​ December 24,2022

​ Professor Smith is looking for a student to join his research program regarding campus sports activities. The duties of the position require that the student collects data on campus sports activities to be analyzed by the research team. This includes observing various athletic events, speaking to student athletes, staff, and faculty,and documenting their experiences.

​ 史密斯教授正在寻找一名学生加入他关于校园体育活动的研究计划。该职位的职责要求学生收集有关校园体育活动的数据，以供研究团队分析。这包括观察各种体育赛事，与学生运动员、工作人员和教职员工交谈，并记录他们的经历。

​ In addition to data collection, the student will also be responsible for organizing meetings with Professor Smith or other research team members to discuss project progress. The successful applicant should be highly organized, patient, and have a keen interest and knowledge in sports.

​ 除了数据收集外，学生还将负责组织与史密斯教授或其他研究团队成员的会议，讨论项目进展。成功的申请人应该组织能力强、有耐心，并且对体育有浓厚的兴趣和知识。

​ If you are interested in joining this research program, please contact us at email@123.com to apply.

​ 如果您有兴趣加入此研究计划，请通过 email@123.com 联系我们进行申请。

​ Students’ Union

​ 学生会

​ Li Ming

​ Shown in the picture is an eye-catching scene of drag-boat race in a Chinese traditional festival. Obviously, the two group members are rowing with all their efforts, in the meantime, a lot of young people on shore are cheering on the race. And there is not be ignored, a comment from elderly couples who are appreciating the more and more wonderful activities in recent years.

​ 图中展示了中国传统节日中引人注目的划船比赛场景。显然，两个小组成员正在全力以赴地划船，与此同时，岸上很多年轻人都在为比赛加油。不容忽视的是，老年夫妇的评论，他们正在欣赏近年来越来越精彩的活动。

​ What behind the picture is clear: the increasing number of younger is participating in and paying more attention to traditional culture and festivals. One can find that the considerable ways of celebrating traditional culture has gone viral on Chinese social media platform, such as Sina Weibo, Xiaohongshu, with millions of users. Also, the education of cultural heritage is welcomed by students from primary level to college students. Our treasure, such as moon cake festival, spring festival, will be explored further if Chinese young generation are active in joining rather than just sending a message or red envelope.

​ 这幅图景的背后是显而易见的：越来越多的年轻人正在参与并更加关注传统文化和节日。人们可以发现，庆祝传统文化的大量方式已经在中国的社交媒体平台上传播开来，例如新浪微博、小红书，拥有数百万用户。此外，文化遗产教育受到从小学到大学生的欢迎。如果中国年轻一代积极参与，而不仅仅是发送信息或红包，我们的宝藏，如月饼节、春节，将得到进一步探索。

​ Of course, our accepted belief— cherished traditional culture, is the essential cornerstone of revitalizing our nation. For the most of the elderly, they would be glad to witness our growth on awareness of our own traditional days, and wait for the coming future, with great confidence. For the most of young generation, we will be willing to receive more traditional cultural education and be a part of these activities.

​ 当然，我们公认的信念—珍视的传统文化，是振兴我们国家的重要基石。对于大多数老年人来说，他们会很高兴见证我们对自己传统生活的认识的成长，并充满信心地等待即将到来的未来。对于大多数年轻一代来说，我们将愿意接受更多的传统文化教育，并成为这些活动的一部分。
